myCIO Wealth Partners

Comprehensive • Independent • Objective


In 2002 the Sarbanes-Oxley Act was enacted. This set new requirements for all public accounting firms. As a result, MyCIO Wealth Partners was formed when both Ernst & Young and PricewaterhouseCoopers decided to exit the Investment Advisory and Wealth Advisory services. MyCIO is now one of the largest completely Independent Wealth Management firms in the country and is a leader in Independent and Objective advice.


MyCIO serves as both the Chief Investment Officer and Chief Financial Officer for many of its clients and has a specialty in Alternative Investments and Fiduciary Liability. We now provide financial counseling services to Former and Current Chairmen, CEOs, and Presidents of 48 Standard & Poors and Fortune 500 Companies.
